You don't have to handle this alone

If you or your child is in danger, or you're having thoughts of hurting yourself or your child, please reach out right now. These lines are free, confidential, and open 24/7.

🚑 Immediate dangerCall 911If someone is at risk of being hurt right now.
💬 988 Suicide & Crisis LifelineCall or text 988For you – overwhelmed, hopeless, or scared of what you might do.
🧸 Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline1-800-422-4453Support and guidance, any time, no judgment.
✉️ Crisis Text LineText HOME to 741741If talking out loud feels like too much right now.
Dira Parent is not a substitute for professional or medical care. The moves here are gentle, everyday support — not therapy, diagnosis, or emergency help. If you're worried about your child's safety or your own, trust that instinct and reach out above.
